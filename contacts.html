Escalation & Concern Pathway

1
Clinical Concern or Patient Safety Issue
Raise immediately with Dr Nafisa Khatoon Hussain (Nominated Individual). Log in the Semble incident register and the Governance Tracker SEA log. For urgent cancer concerns: same-day patient advice and same-day GP letter requesting GP consider 2WW referral.
2
Safeguarding Concern (Adult)
Contact Dr Nafisa Khatoon Hussain (Safeguarding Lead) immediately. Follow LH-001 Safeguarding Adults Policy. Refer to the local authority adult safeguarding team as appropriate. Do not delay referral pending internal discussion if there is immediate risk.
3
Data Breach or Information Security Incident
Notify Dr Mahreen Khan (IG Lead) immediately. Follow DP-005 Data Breach and Incident Response Policy. If a reportable breach, notify the ICO within 72 hours. Involve the external DPO. Log in the Governance Tracker incident log.
4
Complaint
Follow CQS-007 Complaints Policy. Acknowledge within 3 working days. Investigate and respond within 20 working days. Escalate unresolved complaints to the Nominated Individual (Dr NK Hussain). Patients may also contact the CQC or seek independent resolution.
5
Whistleblowing / Raising a Concern
Follow OP-006 Whistleblowing Policy. Concerns may be raised with either director in confidence. If concerns relate to a director, contact the CQC directly on 03000 616161 or via cqc.org.uk.
